MMCTicket Events

MMCTicket Events is a beautiful web application that aims to provide an easiest way to buy concert Ticket.

It's time to have your ticket

Features

Effortless Payment

No need to enter lengthy credit card information. Simply use your mobile money account linked to your phone number for a smooth purchase

Instant Confirmation

Get instant confirmation of your ticket purchase once your mobile money payment is complete. No waiting for approvals or verifications

Enhanced Security

Our secure integration with trusted mobile money providers ensures your financial information stays protected throughout the transaction

Familiar Convenience

Pay for your tickets using the same mobile money platform you use for everyday transactions. It's a familiar and comfortable experience

About me

This project stems from a long-standing passion for live music and the occasional struggle to snag concert tickets. For years, I've navigated the complexities of online ticketing platforms, sometimes missing out due to slow loading times or confusing interfaces.

The idea sparked during a conversation with friends lamenting the same challenges. We envisioned a user-friendly and efficient platform that streamlines the concert ticket buying experience. This project, developed as a Portfolio Project for Alx Africa SWE Programme (https://www.alxafrica.com/), represents an opportunity to bring that vision to life.

While this is a solo project, I am leveraging the wealth of resources and mentorship provided by ALX Africa to build my development skills.
